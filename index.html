Welcome to CHABCON 2026

The Chest & Heart Association of Bangladesh (CHAB) cordially invites you to attend the 43rd Annual General Meeting and International Chest Congress 2026, scheduled for May 5-6, 2026 in Dhaka, Bangladesh.

This premier conference brings together leading experts, researchers, and healthcare professionals from around the world to share cutting-edge research, innovative clinical practices, and emerging trends in cardiology and pulmonary medicine.

Join us for two days of exceptional learning opportunities, networking with peers, and exploring the latest advancements in chest and heart healthcare.

Conference Highlights

International Faculty

Learn from renowned speakers and experts from leading medical institutions worldwide, sharing their knowledge and expertise.

CME Credits

Earn Continuing Medical Education credits while staying updated with the latest advances in cardiology and pulmonology.

Global Network

Connect with healthcare professionals from across the globe and build lasting professional relationships.
